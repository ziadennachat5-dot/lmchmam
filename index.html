The Story

Who is Lmchmam?

The King of Moroccan Family Comedy

Lmchmam — known to millions as the face of عائلة المشمام — is a trailblazer in Morocco's digital comedy scene. Unlike solo stand-up comedians, his unique selling point is ensemble comedy: featuring his real family in sketches that blur the line between reality TV and scripted humor.

His comedy is deeply rooted in Darija (Moroccan Arabic), drawing on the cultural nuances, slang, and everyday absurdities that make life in Morocco hilariously unique. From generational clashes to wedding chaos, his content is a mirror held up to Moroccan domestic life — and the reflection is always funny.

He's part of a new wave of Moroccan entertainers who bypass traditional TV entirely, building direct fan relationships through YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram.
